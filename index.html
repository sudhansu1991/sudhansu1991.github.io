Overview

I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow working in the fields of Natural Language Processing (NLP), Machine Translation (MT), and Data Science. My research focuses on developing AI-powered multilingual systems, with a strong emphasis on low-resource languages and educational technology.

I have published my work in prestigious venues such as ACM TALLIP, Springer, Elsevier, and Cambridge University Press. During my PhD at NIT Rourkela, I contributed to advancing Indic language MT, particularly in low-resource and multilingual contexts.

My broader research interests include Large Language Models (LLMs), language modeling, knowledge extraction, and network analysis for multilingual data. I am passionate about creating inclusive AI technologies that bridge language gaps and empower diverse communities.

Always open to collaboration in: NLP, MT, LLMs, low-resource language tech, and educational AI systems.
