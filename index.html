Research Profile

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher working in Natural Language Processing (NLP), Data Science, and Artificial Intelligence. My work focuses on developing computational methods to analyze and model textual and speech data, enabling intelligent systems for real-world applications.

I have worked on a range of NLP applications, including machine translation, speech processing, text analytics, and multimodal language systems. My research integrates machine learning, language modeling, and data-driven approaches to extract meaningful insights from complex data.

I am particularly interested in applying NLP and data science techniques to domains such as healthcare, legal and policy text analysis, education, and cultural and historical data, with a focus on building practical and impactful AI solutions.

My work has been published in venues including ACM TALLIP, Springer, Elsevier, and Cambridge University Press. During my PhD at NIT Rourkela, I worked on multilingual language technologies and contributed to the development of intelligent NLP systems.

Open to collaboration in NLP, data science, AI applications, and interdisciplinary research involving real-world data.
